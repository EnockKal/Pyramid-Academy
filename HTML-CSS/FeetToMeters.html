Feet to Metter

Definition:

A foot (symbol: ft) is a unit of length in the imperial and US customary systems of measurement. A foot was defined as exactly 0.3048 meters in 1959. One foot contains 12 inches, and one yard is comprised of three feet.

A meter or metre (symbol: m), is the base unit of length and distance in the International System of Units (SI). The meter is defined as the distance traveled by light in 1/299 792 458 of a second. This definition was slightly modified in 2019 to reflect changes in the definition of the second.

How to convert Foot to Meter:

1 ft = 0.3048 m

1 m = 3.280839895 ft

Example:

Convert 15 ft to m

15 ft = 15 * 0.3048 m = 4.572 m
